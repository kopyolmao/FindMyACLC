Image Upload to Google Drive

Setup Instructions

  1. Click the "Authorize Google Drive" button and sign in to your Google account
  2. Grant permission for the app to upload files to your Drive
  3. Select an image by dragging & dropping or clicking "Browse Files"
  4. Click "Save to Google Drive" to upload your image
